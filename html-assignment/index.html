About me

My name is lalili Messaoud ,I am currently studying at Ac I am an ambitious young person with a strong passion for ancient architecture and architectural heritage. I am deeply interested in historical buildings and the stories they tell through structure, proportion, and detail. Traditional architecture inspires me because of its balance between strength, beauty, and function. For this reason, I decided to study design, as it is closely related to architecture and architectural drawing, and it helps me better understand space, form, and visual composition.

My future ambition is to create a strong connection between the solidity and authenticity of ancient architecture and the creativity of modern design. I aim to blend traditional architectural principles with contemporary design techniques to produce work that respects the past while embracing innovation. My goal is to develop designs that preserve architectural identity while responding to modern needs, creating a bridge between historical heritage and modern visual expression,I also believe that learning neverstops 1,and curiosity is the key to progress
